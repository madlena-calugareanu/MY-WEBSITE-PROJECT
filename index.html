01. About Me

I thought I figured it out when I started my English and German language and literature degree, only to find out that sometimes life takes turns without asking for permission. Teaching foreign languages seemed an appropriate career path for a person motivated to be a life-long learner. But it kind of felt too stiff and coordinated, so I chose to be bold and go beyond "traditional languages", by choosing something different.

I found the missing part in Front-End Development, as I started my first web development course. Since this is the field for creativity and problem solving, it sparks my enthusiasm. I am diligent at making designs responsive and eye-catching, as sometimes first impressions do matter. It is quite satisfying to make things work and look amazing. I am enthusiastic about learning new programming languages, frameworks, and skills.
